COMMON QUESTIONS

Is Astra available on mobile?

Yes, Astra is designed for cross-platform performance on both iOS and Android. Mobile-first, performance-driven.

Does it support offline work?

Absolutely. Your data is stored locally and syncs automatically when you're back online. Never lose momentum.

What is "Nag Mode"?

A smart notification feature that nudges you about high-priority missions until they are completed. Your relentless ally.

How does the Shield System work?

Shields are earned through consistency. They automatically activate to protect your streak if you miss a day, keeping your momentum intact.
